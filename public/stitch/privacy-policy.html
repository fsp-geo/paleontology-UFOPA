Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Information We Collect

Our primary goal in collecting information is to provide a curated and personalized experience for researchers. We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, request access to restricted datasets, or subscribe to our scientific bulletins.

person_search

Identifiable Data

Name, institutional affiliation, ORCID iD, and professional email address.

database

Research Data

Search queries, collection bookmarks, and downloaded stratigraphy models.

2. Scientific Ethics & Usage

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is used exclusively to validate researcher credentials and to provide high-resolution archival material that may be restricted by geographic or institutional boundaries.

"The preservation of digital stratigraphic records is a collective responsibility. Data privacy ensures the integrity of the scientific community's collaborative spirit."

3. Security Layers

We implement industry-standard "Sedimentary Security"—layered encryption protocols that protect data at rest and in transit. Access to restricted holotype records requires multi-factor authentication and institutional verification.

Have questions about your rights?

Our compliance team is available to discuss GDPR, LGPD, and scientific ethics compliance.

Terms of Use

Version 2.4.0

1. Intellectual Property

All digital assets, 3D scans, and stratigraphic datasets hosted on the Strata Archive are the intellectual property of Petrobras Paleontology Division or its contributing partners. Users are granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable license for academic research and educational purposes only.

2. Citation Protocols

Correct attribution is vital for maintaining the provenance of scientific discoveries. Any publication utilizing data from this archive must include a formal citation using the following DOI-based format:

[Researcher Name], Strata Archive Digital Repository (2024). Petrobras Paleontology Division. DOI: 10.STRATA/ARCHIVE.2024.01

3. Access Rights & Limitations

Strata Archive reserves the right to suspend or terminate access for any user found to be misusing the data, attempting to bypass security layers, or failing to provide appropriate citation. Commercial exploitation of any archive material without prior written consent is strictly prohibited.

4. Liability Limits

While we strive for absolute geological accuracy, Strata Archive provides all data "as is." We are not liable for any scientific inaccuracies, model variances, or data interruptions caused by system maintenance.

Acceptance of Terms

By continuing to use the Strata Archive, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to these policies and terms.
