Regulatory Framework & Data Governance

Our commitment to stratigraphic data integrity and researcher privacy is foundational to the PaleoPortal Strata Archive System.

1. Terms of Use

1.1

Acceptance of Terms

By accessing PaleoPortal, you acknowledge that you are a verified institutional researcher or authorized partner. Use of the database implies an agreement to maintain the stratigraphic integrity of the datasets and respect the geological heritage documented herein.

1.2

Institutional Licensing

Access is granted on a per-institution basis. Credentials must not be shared beyond authorized personnel. Petrobras ESG guidelines mandate that all research conducted through this portal adheres to international environmental ethics standards.

1.3

Research Ethics

Users must credit PaleoPortal in all derivative research papers. Specifically, any stratigraphic models generated using our "Region" dataset must cite the original excavating institution and the archive's specific DOI.

"The digital archive is as fragile as the fossils it protects."

2. Privacy Policy

Researcher Data Protection

We collect institutional affiliation, research focus, and portal interaction logs. This data is utilized solely for enhancing query performance and verifying compliance with data sovereignty laws. We never monetize researcher metadata.

security Encryption

All dataset transfers are secured via AES-256 encryption. Institutional APIs are restricted via IP-white-listing and rotating cryptographic keys.

history Retention

Inactive researcher accounts and associated log data are purged after 24 months of dormancy to minimize digital footprints.

3. Intellectual Property

The 3D stratigraphic models, high-resolution fossil scans, and proprietary indexing algorithms contained within PaleoPortal are the joint property of Petrobras and its academic partners.

Users are granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to utilize these assets for non-commercial academic research only. Commercial exploitation, including the use of stratigraphic data for independent mineral exploration without explicit written consent, is strictly prohibited.
