Institutional Terms

1. Intellectual Property

Revised May 2024

All digital assets, including 3D fossil scans, high-resolution photography, and stratigraphic maps hosted on the Strata Archive, are the exclusive intellectual property of the Petrobras Paleontology Division and its academic partners.

Reproduction for educational or non-profit research is permitted under the Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0) license, provided clear citation is maintained.

2. Data Sovereignty

Revised May 2024

Strata Archive respects the data sovereignty of national territories. Specimens discovered within sovereign borders are archived with explicit metadata regarding their origin, and digital access is managed in coordination with local archaeological authorities.

3. Researcher Liability

Revised May 2024

Access to the High-Resolution Portal is granted to verified institutional researchers. Strata Archive assumes no liability for scientific conclusions drawn from the data; researchers are responsible for independent verification through peer-review processes.
